Why Partner with Letsgodoit?


Letsgodoit provides a simple mechanism to allow groups of friends to organise to do activities. If you are a website which has content that users can act on then you should be using Letsgodoit. PENSSISISIS

We provide a simple button that users can click to share an item with a select group of facebook friends. The friends get notified on facebook and can then decide to go and do the activity. The users stay on your website (Letsgodoit pops up in a lightbox) allowing the users to continue reading your site.

Do consumers of your website prefer to do the activites on your website on their own, or with their friends? If the answer is "with their friends" then you need Letsgodoit.

Replace the out-of-date "Email to Friends" button with a fully featured social sharing mechanism.
